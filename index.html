Plume Fossil

The Plume Fossil (Japanese: はねのカセキ Feather Fossil) is a Fossil introduced in Generation V that can be regenerated into Archen. It is the counterpart of the Cover Fossil.

Defeatist

Defeatist (Japanese: よわき Timid) is an Ability introduced in Generation V. It is the signature Ability of Archen and Archeops. Defeatist halves the user's Attack and Special Attack stats when their HP is equal to or less than half of its maximum.

Rock Throw

Rock Throw (Japanese: いわおとし Rock Drop) is a damage-dealing Rock-type move introduced in Generation I. Rock Throw deals damage and has no secondary effect. It has an accuracy of 90%.

Wing Attack

Wing Attack (Japanese: つばさでうつ Wing Strike) is a damage-dealing Flying-type move introduced in Generation I. Wing Attack inflicts damage and has no secondary effect. It has a power of 60.

Agility

Agility (Japanese: こうそくいどう High-speed Movement) is a non-damaging Psychic-type move introduced in Generation I. It was TR12 in Generation VIII, and is TM004 in Generation IX. Agility increases the user's Speed by two stages.

Dragon Claw

Dragon Claw (Japanese: ドラゴンクロー Dragon Claw) is a damage-dealing Dragon-type move introduced in Generation III. Dragon Claw inflicts damage and has no secondary effect.
